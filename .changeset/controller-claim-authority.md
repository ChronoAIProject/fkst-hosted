Add the controller's in-memory claim & placement authority — mutual exclusion and session placement move off MongoDB into the single authoritative controller (#135). A `ClaimMap` (Mutex<HashMap> + an AtomicI64 fence) is THE mutual-exclusion gate, replacing the Mongo `leases` unique index + `transition_guarded` CAS: claim is a single locked check-and-insert (conflict → the same 409), set_status is a fence-guarded in-memory CAS, and reassign bumps the fence BEFORE the redo is dispatched so a superseded worker's late writes are fence-rejected. `placement::select_worker` is a 1:1 port of the proven `select_pod` (least-loaded, lowest-id tie-break, max-load cap). A reassignment driver redoes a dead worker's work immediately (abrupt) or after its `Released` ack (graceful, ~zero overlap), with a no-op secret-redispatch seam for #138. The session service gains a `ControllerHandle` placement seam alongside the untouched Mongo path (the live path until #143); the driver-lifecycle status writes move onto the claim map in #136 when engine execution relocates to the worker. Externally observable session/goal semantics — including the 409-on-already-running — are unchanged.
